challenge: |
  Multiple fund strategies operated on separate Excel-based models with little
  standardisation, producing inconsistent calculations for AUM, fund
  waterfalls, IRR, and MOIC. Each fund team duplicated data-preparation work,
  budgeting and scenario cycles required heavy manual consolidation, and the
  spreadsheet sprawl increased the risk of formula errors, version drift, and
  over-reliance on a small group of model owners. Legacy models could no
  longer scale with the firm's product complexity.
solution: |
  We designed and built nine integrated Anaplan models spanning the firm's
  major investment lines — direct lending, public fund strategies, real
  estate secondaries, alternative investment strategies, and regional credit
  platforms — standardising the calculation of key metrics and the
  forecasting process across the organisation. An Anaplan Data Hub houses
  portfolio, fund, and investment-level data as a single governed source of
  truth, feeding each forecasting model. Dynamic scenario planning lets
  users instantiate new forecasts in seconds, clone prior versions as a
  starting point, run side-by-side comparisons, and rapidly stress-test
  market or business changes.
